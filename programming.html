Teaching 2020

Unleashing Young Minds:
Inspiring Python Programming and Digital Creativity

My journey into teaching began at the elementary school level, where I had the joy of introducing K–6 students to the world of computers. I loved inspiring the next generation to explore digital creativity—whether through interactive activities on Code.org or building basic Python programs that sparked curiosity and confidence.

After graduating from SLCC with high honors in Computer Science & Information Systems, I knew I wanted to make an even bigger impact. I designed and launched a first-of-its-kind program that combines computer literacy, foundational technology skills, and certification readiness. Today, I teach courses in Basic Computer Skills (BCS), CompTIA A+, and Security+, helping students of all backgrounds build the technical confidence they need to succeed.

As John C. Maxwell said in The 21 Irrefutable Laws of Leadership: "The secret to success is found in your daily agenda." Every class I teach is an opportunity to build future leaders, one lesson at a time.
